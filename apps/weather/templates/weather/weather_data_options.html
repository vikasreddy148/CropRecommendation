About Weather Data
Why is weather data important?

Weather data helps our AI system provide accurate crop recommendations. The system analyzes:

  • Temperature: Affects crop growth and development
  • Rainfall: Water availability for crops
  • Humidity: Moisture levels in the air
  • Wind Speed: Affects pollination and disease spread
Tips
  • API fetch provides real-time data from weather stations
  • You can fetch weather for fields, farms, or custom locations
  • Forecast data helps plan farming activities
  • Weather alerts notify you of extreme conditions
